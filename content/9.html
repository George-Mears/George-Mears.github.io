Norfolk terrior


Norfolk terriers are a type of small terrier breed with a lot to offer. They are easy to train, loyal and friendly with people and other dogs. They can also be quite independent and don't need a lot of exercise. They are great for families who don't have a lot of time to spend on a dog. Norfolk terriers are also great companions and make wonderful additions to any home. They are intelligent and have the ability to learn quickly, making them great for learning tricks and commands. Overall, Norfolk terriers are a great breed of dog for anyone looking for an intelligent, loyal, and loving pet.
